Executive Summary

The Octa Investment Digital Platform, operated by Octa Investment Group, offers two innovative digital products—Octa Prime and Octa Fractional. Octa Prime provides users with digital profit-sharing rights from premium apartment development projects without transferring direct property ownership. Octa Fractional offers fractional digital shares in Octa Prime portfolios as well as profit-sharing rights from rented apartments equipped with crypto validator nodes.

This documentation provides a comprehensive overview of the Octa Investment Digital Platform. It explains our business model, product offerings, investment structure, user workflows, technical architecture, legal framework, and future roadmap.

Investment Structure & Financial Model

Fee and Tax Structure

  • Property Acquisition Tax: 4.2% of the Market Value.
  • Acquisition Deposit: €5,000 per profit-sharing agreement.
  • Management Fee: 5% per annum (if ROI exceeds 14%).
